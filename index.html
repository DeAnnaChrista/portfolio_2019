About

We're two highschool students fed up with the highschool education system.

The system has far too many issues for us to count so here are our main focus points.

Student Population issues

Hear the reasons whyy we're giving up and why we we're so tired.

Administration

What we think Admin is doing and whether it's helping or harming us.

Treatment of LGBTQ students

Why does your sexuality do you more harm than help in the public education system.

Student Visibility

We feel invisible and things seem to only get done when we scream or bring in parents, we explain why.

Mission statement

We want you our listener to understand what we go through in highschool the stress that drives us crazy, along side the plain old annoyance. We are "children" ,but we want a change we want to preserve our rights. We want to be respected and understood on the same intellectual level as adults. The system has failed us but YOU can help fix it.
